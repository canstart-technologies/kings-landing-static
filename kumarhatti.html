The Location

Why Kumarhatti

Shaped by Serenity

Kumarhatti offers a life free from crowds and high elevation extremes. A serene environment and close-knit community make it ideal for families, retirees, and those seeking a slower pace of life.

Surrounded by Greenery

Pine, deodar, and oak-dotted slopes frame everyday living here. Lush vistas, cool breezes, and seasonal blooms ensure nature remains a constant presence, not an occasional escape.

Well-Connected yet Removed

Kumarhatti’s strength lies in its balance—easy access without loss of peace. Located along NH5, it connects seamlessly to Solan, Kasauli, and major cities, while preserving a quiet, residential character.

Experience Kumarhatti

Moments That Will Stay With You

Misty morning walks along pine-lined roads, unhurried drives between hill towns, quiet moments at roadside cafés, and a climate that remains gentle through the year—life in Kumarhatti is defined by ease and everyday calm.
